Should i invest?

risks

Roughly one-half of small businesses fail within five years. for those that succeed, the time horizon to create value for shareholders tends to be 7+ years. please set your expectations accordingly

You should understand that you may lose your entire investment. If you are risk-averse, are just starting to invest, have only a little money to invest or need the money in the short term, crowdfunding investments likely are not for you

Investments also carry liquidity risk. you will be limited in your ability to resell your investment for the first year – and you may need to hold your investment for an indefinite period of time as no secondary market currently exists

Be sure to read about Specific risks associated with each offering in their Form C
